Modelling Supernovae Lightcurve
Tags
Physics
My dissertation project for M.Sc in Physics, Cardiff University. Supervised by Dr. Cosimo Inserra.
This project aims to produce a computer program based on Nickel-56 mass, ejecta mass, and kinetic energy parameters to model luminosity evolution of supernovae. This will enable us to understand the physical parameters needed to power supernovae and to search for relationships between such parameters to understand the supernova population better.
